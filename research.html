Research Interests

Blind Helper Device

Abstract: This research focuses on developing a device to assist blind individuals in navigating their surroundings. The device uses ultrasonic sound reflection systems to detect obstacles and an Android phone for object recognition. An AI system is integrated to suggest directions and the shortest paths. The device is controlled by an embedded system, and data is processed using vibration motors, where the speed variations in the motors represent processed data.

Optical Fiber Waveguide

Abstract: This research explores the design and functionality of optical fibers as waveguides for light signals. The study delves into the materials and refractive index properties of silica-based optical fibers, including Photonic Crystal Fibers (PCFs). The aim is to design a hexagonal chemical sensor with high relative sensitivity across a wide wavelength range and reduce confinement loss while investigating sensitivity accuracy with fabrication tolerance.

Renewable Energy in Bangladesh

Abstract: This research examines the energy challenges facing Bangladesh and the potential of renewable energy resources. The study highlights the need for sustainable energy solutions, focusing on solar, wind, biomass, and hydro-power as key sources. The research also involves predicting solar and wind energy conversion to electrical energy using machine learning, as well as forecasting future energy demands in Bangladesh.

Current Research Projects

I am currently engaged in predicting the conversion of solar and wind energy into electrical energy for specific regions using machine learning techniques. Additionally, I am working on forecasting future energy demands in Bangladesh to better align national energy planning with sustainable development goals.
